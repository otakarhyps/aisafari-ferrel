What We Covered

The COSTAR Framework

A structured approach to crafting prompts that get you better results:

  • Context – What background does the AI need to understand your situation?
  • Objective – What exactly do you want to achieve?
  • Style – What format or structure should the output have?
  • Tone – What voice or manner should the AI use?
  • Audience – Who is this for?
  • Response – What should the output look like?

Digital System Organization

Setting up your environment for AI success:

  • Bookmarks – Quick access to your AI tools
  • Shortcuts – Keyboard commands and saved prompts
  • Assistants available – Know what tools you have access to

The Key Principles

  • Be fast – Speed matters in building habits
  • Be contextual – Give the AI what it needs to help you
  • Be ready to adjust – Your system needs change every month
